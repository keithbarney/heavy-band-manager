## Description

Band Practice helps your band find the next time everyone's actually free to rehearse — without the group-chat back-and-forth.

Each member connects their calendar. Band Practice quietly figures out who's busy and who's open, then shows you a shared month view: green dots are days everyone's free, yellow dots are days some of you are. Tap a day to see exact windows of overlap.

Schedule rehearsals once and they show up on every member's calendar. Add a location, lock in a time, and your bandmates get the details automatically.

Built for working bands who'd rather play music than negotiate Mondays.

**Features**
• Shared month view with availability dots — see overlap at a glance
• Tap any day to see who's free and when
• Calendar integration — availability is derived from your device calendar, you don't have to enter it manually
• Schedule rehearsals to everyone's calendar in one tap
• Multi-band support — one app, every project you play in
• Location search for venues and rehearsal spaces
• Apple Sign-In, dark mode, no ads, no tracking
